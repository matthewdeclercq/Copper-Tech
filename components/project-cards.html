Michigan

Off-Grid No-Compromise Camper Van

A client envisioned the ultimate off-grid camper van, free from gas generators or propane. We delivered a 24V, 10.5 kWh LFP battery system powered by 800 watts of rooftop solar, with redundant grid and alternator charging for maximum reliability. The system supports major loads like an electric oven, induction cooktop, and air conditioning, as well as numerous small accessories such as LED lighting, USB-powered devices, and satellite connectivity. The result? A camper limited only by food and water supplies.

  • 10.5 kWh LFP Battery Storage
  • 800W Rooftop Solar
  • Full Kitchen Power
